# OGame OptiMine

ROI recommendations for buildings, tech, and Lifeforms in OGame.

## Features
- **Top 100 Investments**: Shows a ranked list of the best ROI opportunities across your empire.
- **Empire Overview**: Detailed breakdown of Mine production, Research bonuses, and Lifeform efficiencies.

## Usage
Click the **OptiMine** button in the OGame sidebar menu to open the dashboard.
